# Static pages show blank screen with no content

## Environment
- Device: Android Samsung Galaxy A52s 5G
- OS Version: 14
- App Version: 3.3.9
- Language: English
- Network: WiFi

## Severity
High

## Priority
High

## Description
Several static informational pages are displayed as blank white screens without any content, preventing users from accessing important information.

## Preconditions
- Application is installed
- User is able to navigate through the app

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Open Go Bus mobile application.
2. Navigate to any of the following pages:
   - Cities and Stations
   - Go Bus News
   - About Company
   - Customer Service
   - Privacy Policy
   - Terms and Conditions
3. Observe the page after it finishes loading.

## Actual Result
The page appears completely blank with no visible text or data.

## Expected Result
The page should display the relevant content properly.
